Refund Policy


  1. No refund of Registration Fee (10% of Course Fee) shall be made. It is towards administrative expenses incurred by the company.

  2. No refund will be made under any circumstances after joining the Course except in case of cancellation of the batch by SKILL MONKS . In case of batch cancellation, the refund amount will be done based on as per pro-data calculations.

  3. If any student pays the fee for any course and wants a refund before the commencement of classes/course in the Institute, all other fees other than Registration Fee will be refunded along with PDCs submitted, if any. Please note that the registration fee will not be refunded.

  4. In case of admissions happened through the partner platforms refund policy is as defined by the partner platform.

  5. If the batch gets canceled after the discussion with management your refund will be processed within 15 days.
